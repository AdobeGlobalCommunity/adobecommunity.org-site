Making content available at the right time, to the right people is essential to the inform, market, sell and support customers, partners and engage employees. Cisco’s diverse global authoring community supports over 90 locales using AEM for its customer, partner and employee base. In this session, Shakeela Yazadi and Murali Vytla will cover Content Sharding (Horizontally Scale), including the business drivers and objectives, a technical deep dive on our approach and discuss the various decisions one needs to make to successfully scale AEM.

  

Murali Vytla, an IT Architect with Cisco.com having 18+ years of industry experience with rich expertise in Personalization, Content Management, and web technologies. He joined Cisco in 2008 and since then worked on all major initiatives of cisco.com like My Cisco, Demand Generation and Web Experience Management platform. He is passionate about technologies and during his free time explores and evaluates new open source technologies that are best suited to develop social awareness mobile apps for non-profit organizations.

 

Shakeela Yazadi is a Senior Solution Analyst in the Cisco Content Service group at Cisco Systems. She worked in various IT functional areas such as Sales, Channel Partners, Marketing, Health Care industry and now on web technologies. In her current role, Shakeela is working closely with Business and other IT groups to enable the next generation Digital Experience and Web Experience Management platform. Her expertise is in Globalization and Personalization of content management. Outside of work she enjoys spending time with her kids and traveling.
