• What we'll do 
ONLINE WEBINAR: Satish Ramade will discuss the topic of Production Content QA and Agile Zone product with the same name. 
The basic RULE of CMS is content development, which is often misunderstood as an application development activity. When working with a CMS, our goal should be to move past development models to a system that caters to content creation in a function-oriented environment. The Agile Zone “Content QA” Adobe Experience Manager Add-On is just such a system.
