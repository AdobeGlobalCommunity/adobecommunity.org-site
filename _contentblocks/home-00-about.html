The Adobe Global Community (AGC) is an independent, member- led group of practitioners of Adobe solutions whose mission is to share knowledge, facilitate learning and enable professional networking around the Adobe technology platform. We facilitate collaboration, education, and events that enable members to share best practices and real-life success stories that can be used to help maximize investments in Adobe solutions.
